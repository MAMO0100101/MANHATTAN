## How to delete your MANHATTAN account

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

### Option 1 — Email (recommended)

1. Send an email to **alatahaid@gmail.com**
2. Use subject line: **Delete my MANHATTAN account**
3. Include the **phone number** you used to register in the app
4. We will delete your account and data within **30 days** and confirm by email


## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove:

- Your name and phone number
- Your challenge predictions and points
- Your gift participation history
- Your login session on our servers

---

## What may be kept temporarily

- Backup copies may remain for up to **90 days** for security and legal compliance, then are permanently deleted.

---

## Data stored on your device

To remove local data immediately:

1. Open **MANHATTAN** on your phone
2. Tap **Logout**
3. Uninstall the app (optional)

Logging out removes the saved login token from your device.
